RoboticMod

RoboticMod

A logo design for RoboticMod. A gaming/meme YouTuber. He wanted the logo to be cute and friendly while keeping a gaming aesthetic. I decided to go for a game cartridge which fit perfectly for his gaming channel.

Inside the game cartridge, you can see gamepad keys for his eyes. The logo needed some personality of its own so It a cute face. The top of the logo has an antenna that resembles a robot.

AR.E

AR.E

AR.E is a graphic designer and Android customizer. He wanted his logo to have his name initials which are "ARE" with a dot in-between R and E. After multiple experiments, I decieded to use reference of an opaque thin paper which worked very well.

Luxury Apperal International

Luxury Apperal International

Luxury Apperal International is a clothing/fashion brand. They wanted their logo to look professional and in a serif font while keeping a clean, minimalist aesthetic. I used whitespace to my advantage and cut down on some details while keeping letters easily readable from afar.

Resay Dota

Resay Dota

Resay is a Dota 2 YouTuber. He wanted his logo to have an extreme gaming aesthetic but still keep it minimalist. In addition to that, he also wanted his both initials "R" and "D" in the logo.

After a couple days of brainstorming and exchanging ideas, we settled on this final design that combines all aspects of his request. Because of the the way the letters were formed, the logo makes a "2" when rotated 90 degrees clockwise.

HRTC

Hyderabad Round
Table Conference

Hyderabad Round Table Conference or HRTC in short, is a debate conference group from Hyderabad, Pakistan. They wanted their logo to be recognizable and versatile so it can be used on various difference places.

Apart from that, they wanted the logo to have wings, representing an open conference for everyone. They also wanted something else to represent a round table. That is what you see on top and bottom of the logo.
